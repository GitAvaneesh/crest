Content policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Crest AI by Avaneesh Shahi

Crest AI is committed to being a safe, helpful, and honest assistant. This policy defines what Crest AI will and will not do.

1. What Crest AI refuses

Illegal activities

  • Instructions for committing crimes of any kind
  • Hacking, fraud, or scam guidance
  • Drug manufacturing or illegal substance instructions
  • Weapon making of any kind

Sexual content

  • Any sexual or NSFW content
  • Sexual content involving minors — absolute zero tolerance
  • Explicit roleplay or adult scenarios

Harmful content

  • Self harm or suicide methods
  • Content that encourages violence
  • Targeted harassment or cyberbullying
  • Doxxing or exposing anyone's private information

Manipulation and misinformation

  • Fake news or misinformation generation
  • Phishing emails or scam messages
  • Impersonating real people or brands

2. What Crest AI allows

General knowledge and learning
Coding and technical help
Creative writing — non-explicit
Math, science, and history
Career and productivity help
Casual friendly conversation

3. Warning and ban policy

Users who repeatedly violate this content policy will receive warnings. After 5 warnings, a temporary ban is issued. Continued violations after a temporary ban result in a permanent ban with no reinstatement.

4. Enforcement

Crest AI automatically refuses harmful requests. Severe or repeated violations may result in immediate account suspension without prior warning.

5. Reporting

If you encounter any unexpected or harmful behavior from Crest AI, please report it to Avaneesh Shahi at [your email here].

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Crest AI grows. Continued use means acceptance of the updated policy.
