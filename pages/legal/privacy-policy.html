Last updated: May 2026  ·  Crest AI by Avaneesh Shahi

This policy explains how Crest AI collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve Crest AI services

3. Data storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase. We do not sell your personal data to any third party under any circumstances.

4. Third party services

Crest AI is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your rights
