How it works

Every message. A carefully
engineered pipeline.

Crest doesn't just forward your message to an AI. It runs every request through a multi-stage system designed for the safest, most reliable response every time.

01

You send a message

Type anything — a question, an idea, a task, a problem. Crest accepts any text input and immediately begins processing your request through the pipeline.

02

Safety GPT checks it

Before anything else happens, Safety GPT OSS 20B via Groq reviews your message in milliseconds. Harmful, illegal, or policy-violating content is blocked immediately with a clear explanation.

03

Gemini 2.0 Flash responds

Your message — along with up to 35 messages of conversation history — is sent to Gemini 2.0 Flash with Crest's system prompt. Gemini generates a helpful, accurate, and concise response.

04

Failover if needed

If Gemini is rate limited, Crest retries with up to 4 rotating Gemini API keys. If all are exhausted, Llama 4 Scout on Groq takes over. If that fails too, Llama 3.3 70B steps in as the final fallback.

05

Your answer arrives

A clean, accurate response appears in your chat. Usage is tracked so your daily limits are respected. The whole process takes milliseconds — you'll barely notice it happened.
